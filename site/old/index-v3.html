Dada Sadananda

“Meditation is not about escaping life — it is about meeting yourself fully, with all the courage and tenderness that requires.”

Yogic monk, meditation teacher, and founder of the educational project and YouTube channel “Meditationsteps.” Dada radiates a rare combination of spiritual depth and warm, grounded humanity.

Practising yoga and meditation for 25 years and teaching since 2000, he has personally guided over 2,000 people into meditation practice. More than 80,000 people have learned the basic technique through his courses. He has trained over 60 instructors.

After 3 years in yogic monasteries in Sweden and India, Dada now holds seminars worldwide. He founded the Centre for Overall Development in Altai.

Didi Ananda Sanjana

“When we practise together, the collective energy carries us deeper than we could ever go alone.”

Yogic nun, meditation teacher, yoga instructor, and certified alternative medicine specialist from Singapore. Didi brings a gentle but powerful presence — equal parts wisdom and warmth.

Practising yoga across five traditions for over 30 years and teaching internationally, she has led detox retreats with over 300 participants and holds certifications from five countries. She blends yoga with aromatherapy, massage, and holistic wellness, sharing her expertise at global conferences.

Retreat Location

Laumžirgių Žemė / Dragonfly Land

A magical retreat center ~25 km north of Vilnius, founded by Baltic sound therapy pioneers. Two beautiful halls (225 m² Ashtar Hall & 100 m² Gaia Dome), cozy cedar and straw-clay guest cabins (~42 sleeping places), a traditional Baltic sauna, spring-water ponds, and a sacred gong garden.

30 min from Vilnius Airport · 2.5 hrs from Riga · 1.5 hrs from Kaunas

Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

No prior experience is needed. Part 1 is open to complete beginners. Our teachers will guide you through every practice — from meditation and yoga postures to the devotional chanting. You will also receive personal meditation instruction suited to your level. Everyone starts somewhere, and we are glad you are here.
A personal lesson is a private, one-on-one session with a teacher (acharya) who introduces you to a meditation technique and, when appropriate, gives you a personal mantra — a sacred sound vibration to focus your meditation. This is one of the most valuable offerings of the retreat and a meaningful step in one’s spiritual journey. No prior knowledge is required.
The retreat is conducted primarily in English with Russian translation available. Our teachers and participants come from various countries, so English serves as the common language.
The price includes: accommodation in cozy cedar and straw-clay guest cabins, all vegetarian meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner), all teachings and practices, personal meditation instruction, gong meditation session, and access to the venue facilities including the traditional Baltic sauna.
Dragonfly Land is approximately 25 km north of Vilnius — about a 30-minute drive from Vilnius airport. If you are driving from Riga, it is approximately 2.5 hours. Parking is available at the venue. We can help coordinate shared transport from Vilnius for those arriving by air or train — just let us know when you register.
The venue has cozy cedar and straw-clay guest cabins with approximately 42 sleeping places. Rooms are shared. Bed linens are provided. The cabins are surrounded by nature — quiet, warm, and comfortable.
All meals are sattvic vegetarian — freshly prepared, nourishing food without meat, fish, eggs, onion, or garlic. Vegan options are available. If you have specific allergies or dietary needs, please mention them in the registration form and we will do our best to accommodate you.
This retreat follows the practices and philosophy of Ananda Marga (“The Path of Bliss”), a holistic spiritual tradition that integrates meditation, yoga, ethical living, and selfless service. It is not affiliated with any organised religion. People of all backgrounds, beliefs, and traditions are warmly welcome.
For yoga and meditation sessions, please wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothes that allow free movement. In the Ananda Marga tradition, modest clothing is preferred during practices — loose trousers or long shorts rather than tight-fitting leggings. Casual comfortable clothing is fine for the rest of the day.
After registering through the form, you will receive payment details by email. Payment is by bank transfer. If you need to cancel, please contact us as early as possible via Telegram (@Virabhava21). We understand plans change and will work with you to find a solution.
