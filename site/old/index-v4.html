Dada Sadananda

Yogic monk, teacher of yoga and meditation. Founder of the educational online project and YouTube channel "Meditationsteps" with over 763,000 subscribers.

Dada has been practicing yoga and meditation for 29 years. Since the year 2000 has been teaching yoga practices. Has given personal initiations and guides in practice more than 2,000 people. Around 470,000 have learned the universal meditation technique through the Basic Course. Has trained more than 80 yoga and meditation instructors.

Spent 3 years in yogic monasteries in Sweden and India. Annually holds seminars across 32 countries. Founder and organizer of the Centre for overall development in Altai.

Author of the book "Dialogues on Meditation" — exploring meditation, yoga and tantra through the lens of personal monastic experience.

Didi Ananda Sanjana

Nun, yoga and meditation teacher, yoga instructor, and certified alternative medicine specialist from Singapore.

Didi has been practicing yoga in five traditions for over 30 years and teaching internationally. She has led detox retreats with over 300 participants and holds certifications from five countries. Passionate about holistic wellness, she blends yoga with aromatherapy, massage, and fitness, sharing her expertise at global conferences.

Where we gather

Retreat Location

Laumžirgių Žemė / Dragonfly Land

A magical retreat center ~25 km north of Vilnius, founded by Baltic sound therapy pioneers. Two beautiful halls (225 m² Ashtar Hall & 100 m² Gaia Dome), cozy cedar and straw-clay guest cabins (~42 sleeping places), a traditional Baltic sauna, spring-water ponds, and a sacred gong garden.
